Disc Golf Basics

Learn how to throw a perfect disc golf shot and improve your skills.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select the Right Disc: Choose a disc suited for your throw type (driver, midrange, or putter).
  2. Grip the Disc: Hold the disc firmly with fingers under the rim and your thumb on top.
  3. Stance and Aim: Stand sideways to your target with your dominant foot slightly back.
  4. Throw the Disc: Pull the disc back and release it in a smooth motion, aiming for a straight flight path.
