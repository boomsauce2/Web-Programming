Useful Links

Here are some of my favorite resources for programming, disc golf, and outdoor adventures. These sites inspire and help me grow in my interests and skills.

Programming Resources

  • MDN Web Docs - A comprehensive resource for learning web development, from beginner to advanced topics.
  • W3Schools - A great site for learning the basics of web programming with interactive tutorials.

Disc Golf Sites

  • PDGA (Professional Disc Golf Association) - The go-to site for tournament schedules, player rankings, and official rules.
  • Infinite Discs - My favorite store for disc golf gear, including discs, bags, and accessories.
  • Disc Golf Network - Watch live and recorded coverage of professional disc golf tournaments.

Outdoor Adventure Blogs

  • AllTrails - Discover new hiking trails and outdoor adventures with detailed reviews and maps.
  • REI Expert Advice - Get tips and gear recommendations for camping, hiking, and other outdoor activities.
  • onX Maps - A powerful mapping tool for planning outdoor activities, including hiking and hunting.
